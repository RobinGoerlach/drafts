Travel

Travel is less about collecting sights and more about noticing how places actually feel: routes, habits, practical details and small differences in everyday life.

Some places are tied to my own history, others have become recurring destinations over time. This section brings those strands together.

United Kingdom

The UK is not just a destination for me. I was born in Carmarthen in South Wales and still have family in Wales, Scotland and England. That makes this part of the site more about lived connection than sightseeing.

France

Germany

Germany belongs here as well: Hessen, Thuringia, the Elbe Sandstone area or Upper Franconia with factory outlets, smoked beer in Bamberg and seasonal carp around Neustadt an der Aisch.
