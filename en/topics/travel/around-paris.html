Around Paris

Paris does not stop at the city boundary. The wider area matters more than many visitors expect.

Clamart and Meudon

Useful and calmer bases for staying close to Paris without being swallowed by it.

Ivry, Montreuil, Pantin

More everyday, more urban and often more revealing than a purely anonymous hotel belt.

Saint-Germain-en-Laye and Parc de Sceaux

Good options when the wider Paris region should feel greener or more classical.
