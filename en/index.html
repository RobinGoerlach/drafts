Welcome

Robin Görlach

Technology, projects and topics that have become important to me over time.

On this site I collect work, notes and thoughts from IT, Linux, administration, programming, electronics, databases, data analysis and other areas.

There are also topics that are not directly technical, but have stayed with me over the years.

Quite a bit has come together here — perhaps there is something useful or interesting in it, for others as well as for myself.

What this is about

The main focus is on IT, Linux, administration, programming, electronics, databases, data analysis and related technical topics. Alongside that are subjects that grew out of everyday life, practical interest or a longer engagement with particular questions. Over time, other areas have found their place as well. Not everything fits into the same drawer, but that is often what makes collections like this useful.

IT & Linux

Work, notes and reflections on systems, administration, security, infrastructure and the things that simply need to function reliably in day-to-day life.

Software & Programming

Thoughts, examples and projects around development, architecture, tools, data and the attempt to build things that remain understandable and maintainable.

Electronics & Technology

Topics from electronics, hardware and technical contexts that have accompanied me for a long time and often meet software again in one way or another.

Everyday Life, Practice & Other Topics

Over time, other areas have found their place too, such as cooking, plants, gardening or travel — things that are not separate from technology, but belong just as much to life.

Where some of this comes from

Much of what is on this site did not appear all at once, but accumulated over a longer period of time. Some of it comes from projects, some from professional contexts, other parts from curiosity, practical need or simply from the wish to understand things a little better.

Over time, this has brought together not only work and technical notes, but also tools, reflections and topics that I return to again and again.
