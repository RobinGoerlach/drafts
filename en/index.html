Welcome

Robin Görlach

Technology, projects and topics that have become important to me over time.

On this site I collect work, notes and thoughts on IT, Linux, administration, programming, electronics, databases, data analysis and other areas.

There are also topics that are not directly technical, but have stayed with me over the years all the same.

Quite a bit has accumulated here — perhaps some of it will be useful or interesting, both for others and for myself.

What this site is about

At the centre are IT, Linux, administration, programming, electronics, databases, data analysis and related technical topics. Alongside that are subjects that grew out of everyday life, practical interest or spending time with certain questions over the years. Other areas have found their place here as well. Not everything belongs in the same drawer, but that is often what makes such collections useful.

IT & Linux

Work, notes and reflections on systems, administration, security, infrastructure and the things that need to function reliably in everyday life.

Software & Programming

Thoughts, examples and projects around development, architecture, tools, data and the attempt to build things that remain understandable and maintainable.

Electronics & Technology

Topics from electronics, hardware and technical contexts that have accompanied me for a long time and keep touching software in one way or another.

Everyday life, practice & other topics

Over time, other areas have found their place as well, such as cooking, plants, gardening or travel — things that are not separate from technology, but belong to life just as much.

Where some of it comes from

Much of what is on this site did not appear all at once, but accumulated over a longer period. Some of it comes from projects, some from professional contexts, some from curiosity, practical need or simply the wish to understand things better.

Over time, this has brought together not only work and technical notes, but also tools, reflections and topics that I keep returning to.

And otherwise
